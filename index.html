Yard Sale Shopping Tips for Elkin NC

Best Shopping Times

Start early (7-8 AM) for best selection. Visit 11 AM-1 PM for better prices as sellers want to clear items.

Bring Small Bills

Bring $1, $5, $10, and $20 bills. Most Elkin yard sales don't have change for large bills.

Negotiate Respectfully

Polite haggling is expected. Offer 50-70% of asking price, especially when buying multiple items.

Plan Your Route
